Simple Strategy

  • 1. Fill half your plate with colorful vegetables at every meal. This boosts fiber and nutrients without the calories.
  • 2. Choose protein with every meal, like grilled chicken or fish, to keep you full and satisfied throughout the day.
  • 3. Replace sugary snacks with whole fruits or nuts that are easy to grab and provide lasting energy.

Common Mistakes

  • 1. Skipping meals and then overeating later, which can lead to weight gain.
  • 2. Relying on quick convenience foods that may seem healthy but are packed with hidden sugars and unhealthy fats.
  • 3. Not planning ahead, leaving you vulnerable to last-minute unhealthy choices.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I eat healthy on a busy schedule?

Focus on meal prepping on weekends for easy grab-and-go options during the week, like portioned salads or protein packs.

What if I don’t like vegetables?

Try mixing vegetables into smoothies or find easy ways to incorporate them into dishes you already enjoy.

Can I still eat out while trying to lose weight?

Absolutely! Look for grilled options or salads with dressing on the side, and don’t hesitate to ask for modifications.
